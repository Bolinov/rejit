(3) Regexp: ([complex]|(regexp)){2,7}abcdefgh(at|the|[e-nd]as well)
This illustrate the "fast forward" mechanism used in rejit.
With this mechanism, the matching performance of a regexp will be similar to the matching performance of its dominating element chosen by the fast forward mechanism (exceptions exist for yet unhandled cases like .*). This holds as long as the complexity of the regular expression does not overpower the fast forward performances, which can happen when matching a complex regexp in a text with a high density of (potential) matches.
